Understanding Your Results

We show you 4 simple metrics to help you pick the greenest route

🏷️

Emission Grade

Your vehicle gets a simple letter grade (A, B, C, or D) based on how clean it is. Think of it like a report card for your car's emissions.

AVery Clean — Excellent choice
BPretty Clean — Good choice
CAverage — Could be better
DHigh Emissions — Not ideal
📊

Eco-Score

A simple 0-100 score for each route. Higher is better. We look at three things:

  • CO₂ emissions (most important — 50%)
  • Distance (30%)
  • Traffic (20%)
80-100 Excellent 60-79 Good 40-59 Fair 0-39 Poor
🌍

Environmental Cost

This shows the real money value of the pollution your trip creates. In India, every kg of CO₂ costs about ₹1 in environmental damage.

If your trip produces 5 kg CO₂ = ₹5 environmental cost
If your trip produces 15 kg CO₂ = ₹15 environmental cost
